## Bio

![Headshot of Oliver Noteware](./images/oliver-noteware.jpeg)[^oliver-photo-source]

Oliver Noteware is listed as Founder and CEO of [Street Smarts VR](../org/street-smarts-vr.md), where the company frames its origin around solving training-access bottlenecks for small units.[^oliver-about]

The founder section on Street Smarts describes him as a former Marine infantry officer and ties that operational background to the company's readiness-focused product direction.[^oliver-about]

A VRARA podcast listing from 2018 introduces Oliver as Co-founder and CEO, alongside [Alice Formwalt](../person/alice-formwalt.md), describing Street Smarts VR as focused on immersive training for law enforcement and public safety.[^oliver-vrara]
