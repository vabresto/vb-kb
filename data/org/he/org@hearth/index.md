# Hearth

## Snapshot

- Why this org matters: Hearth builds software for home improvement contractors and publicly reports a large contractor user base.[^hearth-team]
- What they do: Hearth describes itself as an all-in-one app for contractor sales workflows including estimates, financing, contracts, and invoicing.[^hearth-about]
- Best way to approach: Lead with contractor acquisition, close-rate, and financing-conversion outcomes.

## Bio

Hearth's about page says the company was founded in 2018 by Anthony Ghosn and Nikhil Pai and is based in Austin and San Francisco.[^hearth-about]

Hearth's team page states the platform serves tens of thousands of contractors.[^hearth-team]

A Hearth announcement published on October 17, 2022 describes the launch of cash-back banking with Marqeta and quotes CEO [Misha Tsidulko](../../../person/mi/person@misha-tsidulko/index.md) on the initiative.[^hearth-banking-newswire]
