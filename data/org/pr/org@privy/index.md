# Privy

## Snapshot

- Why this org matters: Core wallet-infrastructure company connected to [Asta Li](../../../person/as/person@asta-li/index.md) and [Henri Stern](../../../person/he/person@henri-stern/index.md), with broad footprint across crypto application onboarding and key-management workflows.[^privy-careers]
- What they do: Privy positions itself as wallet infrastructure built for scale, with tooling to onboard, activate, and manage users securely.[^privy-home]
- Best way to approach: Lead with concrete user-conversion goals, threat-model requirements, and operational needs for secure wallet orchestration.

## Bio

Privy describes itself as wallet infrastructure for teams building on crypto rails, emphasizing onboarding, key management, and integration layers.[^privy-home][^privy-features]

Its careers page says the company is NYC-based with offices in New York and San Francisco and that it was founded by Asta Li and Henri Stern.[^privy-careers]

Privy's March 2025 funding announcement says its total capital raised exceeded $40M.[^privy-fundraise]

Privy's June 11, 2025 company announcement states that [Stripe](../../st/org@stripe/index.md) is acquiring Privy and that Privy would continue operating as an independent product and team.[^privy-acq]
