# Street Smarts VR

## Snapshot

- Why this org matters: Prior operating context for [Alice Formwalt](../../../person/al/person@alice-formwalt/index.md), and a recurring node for defense/public-safety immersive training.
- What they do: Build VR/MR training systems and mission-specific scenarios for military and public-safety organizations.[^ssvr-home][^ssvr-about]
- Best way to approach: Frame conversations around measurable readiness outcomes, contract pathways, and scenario customization requirements.

## Bio

Street Smarts VR (Street Smarts VR Inc.) is a New York-headquartered immersive training company founded in 2017.[^ssvr-about][^ssvr-sec-2019]

The company reports 50+ agencies, 100+ locations, and 10,000+ users on its website and lists offices in New York, Texas, Florida, and Toronto.[^ssvr-home][^ssvr-about]

Case-study and military-public sources describe an AFIMSC partnership through an AFWERX challenge process for virtual Combat Support Training Range (vCSTR) work, and an earlier Air Force contract announcement tied to AFGSC Security Forces.[^ssvr-case][^ssvr-dvids][^ssvr-globenewswire]
